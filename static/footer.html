Accessible Technology

While opensource has done away with the licensing fees of software, it's deployment and management is still very costly. Ellobed takes care of these backend operations for you so that your technology infrastructure is cost effective, manageable and easy to work with.

How exactly do we do that?

  1. By helping you manage your cloud infrastructure or getting your existing services on cloud.
  2. By giving you tools that can help your sales and marketing department create targeted booking websites on their own.
  3. By deploying applications that help you manage your inventory, your people and your clients all from one place.

And we do all of this on your data center so that there is no lock in.
